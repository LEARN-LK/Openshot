##  **4. Importing Media**

1. Click the **`+` Import Files** button or drag files directly into the **Project Files** area.
2. Supported media: MP4, AVI, MOV, MP3, WAV, PNG, JPG, etc.
3. Drag imported files to the **Timeline**.
